One platform gives you the control to provide meals in multiple ways

Whether you want to give employees a monthly meal stipend or cover the cost of a single meal, our flexible suite of solutions has you covered.

Meal programs

Create weekly or monthly meal stipends for your employees, who can then order on Uber Eats. It's easy to set restrictions for time of day, location, and meal budget.

Meal planning

Put in-office meals on autopilot for your whole team. Use the meal planning feature on UberEats.com to schedule recurring group orders and have your employees add their favorite options.

Gift cards

Show your appreciation for clients and employees with Uber gift cards for rides and meals that will never expire.

Vouchers

Cover the cost of a single meal by sending employees or clients a meal voucher to be redeemed on Uber Eats. You only pay for what is used.
